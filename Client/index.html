Frequently Asked Questions

What is Phantom?

Phantom is a dedicated team within ZeroOne Club focused on hands-on projects, learning new technologies, and fostering a culture of innovation outside of traditional academic settings.

What is ZeroOne Club?

ZeroOne Club is a vibrant community of aspiring developers, designers, and innovators passionate about technology and creativity.

What does Phantom do?

Phantom members work on a variety of projects spanning different domains, including web development, mobile apps, software applications, hardware projects, and more. They collaborate, learn, and innovate together, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in technology.

How does Phantom contribute to ZeroOne Club?

Phantom enriches the ZeroOne Club community by providing opportunities for members to apply their skills in real-world projects, gain practical experience, and learn from each other's expertise. The projects and initiatives undertaken by Phantom contribute to the overall growth and development of the club.
